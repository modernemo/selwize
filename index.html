About Me

Руслан Selwize is a hip-hop artist from Nukus, Uzbekistan who began his musical journey in winter 2023, inspired by the Russian rap scene. His music stands out for its uniqueness and integrity, aiming to convey deep emotions through his tracks.

While Selwize hasn't yet achieved major recognition, he's actively developing his career, releasing music on platforms like Instagram, YouTube, SoundCloud, and Spotify. Follow his work to be among the first to hear new tracks and experience the distinctiveness of his style!
